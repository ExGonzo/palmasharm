Food & places to visit

Simple favorites and scenic spots around Sharm El Sheikh.

Restaurants

Fares Seafood (Old Market)

Fresh local fish, grilled and fried—popular with visitors and residents.

El Masrien (Old Market)

Egyptian dishes like kofta, grilled chicken, and tahini salads.

SOHO Square options

Lots of family-friendly places and an evening promenade with music.

Places to see

Naama Bay

Beachfront walkway, cafés, and relaxed atmosphere—nice at sunset.

Old Market & Al Sahaba Mosque

Colorful market area and a striking mosque near the square.

El Fanar Lighthouse

Coastal views and easy access to snorkeling-friendly beaches nearby.

Simple tips

  • Carry small cash for taxis and markets.
  • Hydrate well; the sun is strong even in cooler months.
  • For snorkeling, consider reef-friendly sunscreen.
